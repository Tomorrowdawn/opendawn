## Conventions

- **ID**: `M-N` (zero-padded, monotonic). Never reused.
- **Status**: `draft | WIP | completed | deprecated`.
- **Builds**: what this milestone constructs, at the design level. 1–3 sentences.
- **Stops**: the observable criterion that makes this milestone done.
- **No Links field**: issues point to milestones via their `milestone:` field. Run `list.py --milestone M-N` to see related issues.
